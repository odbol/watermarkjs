It would be even more handy to download a bunch of watermarked image once generated. Doing so in the client is a great idea, especially if you are uncertain about image library support in a hosting environment. JavaScript is decent at doing things concurrently as well, so you can even make your application snappier by letting the client upload multiple images concurrently instead of a hosting environment that may or may not support such things.
